ABOUT THE PLATFORM

A private workspace with a public commitment to transparency.

Reel Lab is a private content-planning and analytics workspace for Dr. Julian Prosia. It organizes evidence-informed ideas, scripts, filming plans and performance patterns for educational Instagram Reels and TikTok videos. This public site explains the service and its data practices; it does not expose the private dashboard or connected-account data.

01

Build concise eye-health and systemic-health concepts supported by relevant medical literature.

Prepare bulk filming sessions with practical hooks, visual sequences and educational script maps.

03

Learn from performance

With account-owner permission, compare supported metrics to identify useful topics and formats.

CREATOR-AUTHORIZED DATA

Your password is never entered into Reel Lab.

Instagram and TikTok connections use each platform’s official authorization process. Access is read-only and limited to permissions approved by the account owner.

Connections may be revoked, and Reel Lab does not sell personal information.
