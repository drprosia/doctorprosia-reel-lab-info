Privacy Policy

Last updated July 20, 2026

Purpose

Doctor Prosia Reel Lab uses creator-authorized Instagram and TikTok data to help Dr. Julian Prosia understand the performance of his own educational content and plan future videos.

Data accessed

With permission, Reel Lab may access account identifiers, profile statistics, media metadata and platform-available metrics such as followers, views, reach, likes, comments, shares, saves and watch time. Each platform provides a different subset.

Authorization and security

Connections use official Meta and TikTok authorization. Reel Lab does not request or store social-media passwords. Application credentials, access tokens and refresh tokens are encrypted.

Use and sharing

Connected data is used only for the creator’s content planning and performance analysis. Reel Lab does not sell personal information or use connected data for advertising.

Deletion

Connections may be disconnected and revoked at any time. See Data Deletion.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests may be sent to drprosia@ophthalogix.ca.
